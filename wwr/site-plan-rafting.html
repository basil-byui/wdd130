Overview

Purpose

If you want to experience all of the outstanding things South Africa has to offer in a single sightseeing outing, Come to KwaZulu-Natal Province and enjoy white water rafting with your family. Experience the town of Durban on this unique city adventure with clear blue skies, the wind in your hair and a splash or two from the river below.

Audience

Our rafting trips are the ultimate getaway for the whole family as well as a much-needed escape from the fast-paced pressures of city life and to get in touch with the heart of nature. The climate is hot and the water is warm. This outdoor experience is breathtaking. You have the chance to enjoy the family friendly camp sites where you can relax with a beautiful river view. We offer breakfast and lunch while you take your time to experience the serene surounding of nature with a variety of birds and animals.
